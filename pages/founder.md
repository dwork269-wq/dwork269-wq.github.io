# Hi! I'm Rahul!

I'm a Product Manager in the payments industry. Got into the stablecoins space and in early 2025, took the leap and founded Miruvor to really build and contribute.

It all started with the DebtCoin token that I designed to fund an inflation-resistant stablecoin pegged to the US national debt and serve as a critique of the United States' fiscal policy.

Since then, it's been an interesting ride.

I set up StableResearch.xyz to explore stablecoins through data, tools and original research and then launched Medici.ac, a peer-to-peer platform where donors fund verified students directly on-chain, laying the foundation for human capital to become a fundable asset class.

From a solo founder working late into the night, we've grown into a small, enthusiastic team with a real-world-first approach. We've secured grants to support our work and help us keep building!

I also maintain a [blog](/blog) on this site to share top-of-mind thoughts. They're often not finished products, just notes that help in the thinking and learning process.
